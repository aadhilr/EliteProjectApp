Add new Basecamp 2 attachments to Google Drive

When you've got files moving through your task list, it pays to have backups. With this Basecamp 2-Google Drive integration active on your dashboard, app will automatically create a new file on Google Drive for every new attachment detected on Basecamp 2, copying all your material as it gets uploaded.

How It Works

    A new file is uploaded to Basecamp 2

    App automation creates a new Google Drive file

What You Need

    Basecamp 2 Account  

    Google Drive Account
